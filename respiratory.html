呼吸系统疾病 | Respiratory Diseases

概述 | Overview

呼吸系统疾病通常与空气中细菌和霉菌有关，可能导致严重的健康问题。
Respiratory diseases are often related to bacteria and mold in the air, potentially leading to serious health issues.

症状 | Symptoms

  • 咳嗽 | Coughing
  • 呼吸急促 | Shortness of breath
  • 胸痛 | Chest pain

治疗方法 | Treatment

治疗呼吸系统疾病的方式包括：
Treatments for respiratory diseases include:

  • 使用吸入器 | Using inhalers
  • 抗生素治疗 | Antibiotic treatment for bacterial infections
  • 避免刺激物 | Avoiding irritants and allergens
  • 就医检查 | Seeking medical attention for persistent symptoms
