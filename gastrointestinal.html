胃肠道疾病 | Gastrointestinal Diseases

概述 | Overview

胃肠道疾病可能由不良的饮食习惯和环境污染引起，影响消化系统的健康。
Gastrointestinal diseases can be caused by poor dietary habits and environmental pollution, affecting digestive health.

症状 | Symptoms

  • 腹痛 | Abdominal pain
  • 腹泻 | Diarrhea
  • 恶心和呕吐 | Nausea and vomiting

治疗方法 | Treatment

Treatments for gastrointestinal diseases include:

  • 调整饮食 | Adjusting diet
  • 补充水分 | Staying hydrated
  • 使用药物 | Using medications
  • 就医检查 | Seeking medical attention for persistent symptoms
