霉菌感染 | Mold Infections

概述 | Overview

霉菌感染通常由潮湿环境中的霉菌引起，可能导致皮肤、呼吸道等问题。
Mold infections are typically caused by mold in damp environments, potentially leading to skin, respiratory, and other issues.

症状 | Symptoms

  • 皮肤发红、瘙痒 | Redness and itching of the skin
  • 呼吸困难 | Difficulty breathing
  • 过敏反应 | Allergic reactions

治疗方法 | Treatment

治疗霉菌感染的方式包括：
Treatments for mold infections include:

  • 使用抗真菌药物 | Using antifungal medications
  • 保持环境干燥 | Keeping the environment dry
  • 定期清洁 | Regular cleaning to remove mold
  • 就医检查 | Seeking medical attention if symptoms persist
